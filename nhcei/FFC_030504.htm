The First Friday Café, in conjunction with The New Haven Center for Economic Interpretation, presents

 

 

 

Organizing and Politics in New Haven

An Evening of Conversation and Music

 

 

 

·        Gilbert Skillman, professor of Economics, Wesleyan University, will speak on “The Progressive Voice in Contemporary American Politics: No Left Turns Allowed?”

 

·        Jennifer Klein, professor of History, Yale University, presents “For All These Rights, We've Just Begun to Fight:  New Haven and the Legacies of 1199's Historic Struggle to Organize Health Care”

 

 

Join us afterwards for refreshments and radical folk music with Gil Skillman.  Feel free to bring your instrument. 

 

Sponsored by the First Friday Café and The New Haven Center for Economic Interpretation.  Contact us at www.newhavencenter.org.
