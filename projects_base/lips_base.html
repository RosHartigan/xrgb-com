SyncLips

1994 the Children's Television Workshop hired me to design and develop an audio & animation synching application in C for the Macintosh, called SyncLips. The utility analyzed audio data, determining where changes in volume might indicate an animation change.  Animators could then assign one of a series of frames to the audio at any point.
