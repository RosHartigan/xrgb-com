Speech Recognition Prototype

In 1999, Scholastic, Inc. hired me to develop a prototype for a speech recognition product, in which they tested the idea of using speech recognition technology to give students feedback on their reading skills.  I created this product for Windows, using C++, IBM's ViaVoice engine, and Lernout and Hauspie's engine.

A windows-based prototype for a speech recognition product which I developed for Scholastic, Inc., using C++, IBM's ViaVoice engine, and Lernout and Hauspie's voice recognition engine.
