ExploreLearning GizmoTM Development

ExploreLearning hired me in 2003 to program a series of GizmosTM to support their middle school and high school Math e-texts. GizmosTM are shockwave modules that provide a simulation or model of a mathematical concept. I programmed them in Director, using ExploreLearning's proprietary code library.
