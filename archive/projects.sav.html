Projects

 

Scholastic

I recently completed a series of projects for Scholastic, Inc.  These included the Scholastic Phonics Readers, a 12 CD set of 72 interactive stories and activities.  This was followed by the Scholastic Phonics Booster Books, an 8 CD set of additional readers and activities.  The software was created in Director.

 

M@xracks

In 1999 I ported www.maxracks.com to a Mac to a unix server, recreating the functionality in perl and mSQL.  I recently upgraded their website to new versions of perl modules and the database.

 

Flat

Boing!
